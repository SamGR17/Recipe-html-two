Pancakes


Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose or plain flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg

Steps to Prepare

  1. In a bowl, combine together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Make a well in the centre and add the milk, slightly cooled melted butter, vanilla extract and egg.
  2. Whisk the wet ingredients together first before slowly folding them into the dry ingredients. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a nonstick pan or griddle over low-medium heat and wipe over with a little butter to grease pan
  4. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan and spread it out into a round shape
  5. When the underside is golden and bubbles begin to appear on the surface, flip with a spatula and cook until golden. Repeat with remaining batter.

Optional Toppings or Variations

  • Add chocolate chips, blueberries or anything similar while cooking the pancakes
  • Add honey, maple syrup, fruit, butter, ice cream, or frozen yoghurt on top
